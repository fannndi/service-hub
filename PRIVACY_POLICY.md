# Privacy Policy — Service Me

**Last updated:** June 26, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Service Me ("the App") is a gadget repair marketplace platform operated by TI23A4. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
- **Phone number**: Used for account registration, login, and communication via WhatsApp
- **Full name**: Used to identify you within the platform
- **Device address**: Used for courier pickup services
- **Profile photo**: Optional, for account personalization

### 2.2 Service Information
- **Device details**: Brand, model, and type of gadget you submit for repair
- **Damage descriptions and photos**: Used by repair shops to diagnose issues
- **Payment information**: Payment method and transaction records (we do not store full payment card details)

### 2.3 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device model and OS version**: For app compatibility and analytics
- **App usage data**: To improve user experience
- **IP address**: For security and fraud prevention

## 3. How We Use Your Information

| Purpose | Data Used |
|---------|-----------|
| Account creation and login | Phone number, name |
| Service booking | Name, phone number, device information, address |
| Order tracking and notifications | Phone number (via WhatsApp) |
| Payment processing | Payment method, transaction amount |
| Customer support | Name, phone number, order details |
| Service improvement | Aggregated usage data |

## 4. Data Sharing

We share your information only with:

- **Repair shops**: Your name, phone number, and device details are shared with the shop you select for service
- **Supabase**: Our backend infrastructure provider (see [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy))
- **WhatsApp (Fonnte)**: For sending order notifications via WhatsApp
- **Midtrans**: For payment processing (see [Midtrans Privacy Policy](https://midtrans.com/privacy))

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase PostgreSQL servers located in Southeast Asia. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

- Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Row-Level Security (RLS) policies on all user data
- Passwords hashed using bcrypt
- Guest account credentials encrypted using AES-256-GCM

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: View your personal data stored in the app
- **Correction**: Update your name, address, and profile information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and data
- **Portability**: Request a copy of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

## 10. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your data rights, contact us at:

**Email**: ti23a4@serviceme.app
